Food Outlets


By this I would like to share my experience with different varieties of food I ate in Lucknow

For Now I am only covering the outlets which I like the most

For Breakfast

Bajpayee kachodi Bhandar

It is located in Hazratganj near Cathedral School.It is a top player in category Street food and mostly it is famous for its kachoris.Whole day it has a long line of food lovers.


Shamra Ji Ki Chai

If you are a TEA LOVER then u definately shouldn't miss this outlet.It is located in Lalbagh.It is famous for its chai,bun makkhan and samosa


For Chaat Lovers

Royal Cafe

I personally liked it's most famous basket chaat.The basket chaat was highly recommended and it lived up to the hype.The spicy sweet flavour of the Basket chaat ny Royal Cafe are loved by all.


Pandit Chaat Corner

It is located near Naka Chauraha . This my personal fav for any type of chaat and its pani puris aka golgappe are just damm good.Must Try.


For Dinner

Moti Mahal

It is located in Hazratganj. My most fav thing is their Special ThaliI found experience here alot better than other affiliated restaurants


Bombay Pav Bhaji

It is located in Hazratganj.If you are looking for Pav bhaji,this is the place for you.It is served with buttery pavs and spicy bhaji that will just elate you


Cappuccino Blast

It has great Indian and Chinese food along with delicious Starters . If you looking for a place for Family dinner or even day out with your friens,then this is the place.The plus point It has that is has a great view where you can have some great photos


For Deserts

Prakash ki Mashoor Kulfi

One of the finests Kulfi's in Lucknow which you wouldn't likw to miss.It is loacted in Aminabad.It is serving Faluda Kulfi in Lucknow since ages and nothing has beat them.


Ram Asrey

Makhan Malai It is a winter sweet sold in lucknow .It is almost as light as air,fluffy but creamy and intense.It is most famous in Chowk.Never miss this in Lucknow.

Moti Mahal

Again Moti Mahal is famous for its sweets made in desi ghee.Also the Faluda kulfi served here are one of my Favs


Non Veg Outlets

Lucknow is famous for its Mughlai Food

Tunday Kababi

It is the most famous nonveg outlet in Lucknow.It is serving us for more than 100 years.It is famous for its Galouti Kababs which is soo soft that it melts in your mouth along with Mughlai Paratha.Also its Biryani is no less than others.People from all over the World visit here.


Idris Biryani

If you are looking for some mouth water Biryani,then this is the pplace for you.Many Biryanis are famous in the city but this comes first


Nn Veg Point

It is located in Chowk.It is one of the best place if u want to have some delicious "Chicken Masala".


There are many other good non veg outlets such as Kareem Kababi, Dastarkhwan, Wahid Biryani etc

This was my personal opinion And For me No food in India is better than Lucknow in all aspects

Hope This helped you in searching for your next food destination in Lucknow
